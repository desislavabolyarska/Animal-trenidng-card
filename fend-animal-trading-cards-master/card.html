Lion

A lion’s roar can be heard from as far as 5 miles away.

  • Scientific Name:: Panthera leo
  • Lifespan:: 10 – 14 years (Adult, In the wild)
  • Conservation status:: Vulnerable (Population decreasing)
  • Mass:: Male: 190 kg (Adult), Female: 130 kg (Adult)

Living in the grasslands, scrub, and open woodlands of sub-Saharan Africa, the lion is the second largest cat in the world.
